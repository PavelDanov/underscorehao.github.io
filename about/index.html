About me
2020-03-20 16:45

Hey there, I’m Pavel Danov a.k.a. _hao or underscoreHao (on websites that don’t support underscores). Welcome to my personal website. Here you can find about things that interest me and occupy my attention most of the time.

I’m originally from Bulgaria but moved to London in 2018.

My interests are too broad to list, but some honorable mentions - martial arts (been practicing for more than 20 years), film, reading books (a lot of history, politics and science).

Most people that know me are aware of my love for languages, having studied to varying degrees of success - English, German, Russian and now learning Chinese. 我认识你很高兴！

Software Engineer

I’ve been occupied in the software industry professionally for a decade and I’ve been dabbling with computers since I was a child.

I like to play games and I am also fond of bashing my head against the wall when trying to make them. I’m the founder of a small game development studio and have a couple of projects in the pipeline including creating my own game engine.

I’m also available on a contract basis. If you think I can help you with a project you can use the contact methods above to get in touch with me.
