Bookshelf

Here, you’ll find a list of books I’m currently reading or have already finished, along with my thoughts on them. I typically read multiple books at once, distinguishing between recreational reading and reading for learning. When studying a book, I take notes, pay close attention, and complete the exercises and drills, depending on the subject.

Currently Reading

Pro .NET Memory Management (2nd edition)

Having read the first edition of this book some years ago I was happy to find out that a 2nd edition was coming covering .NET 8 and I bought the moment it became available! It’s not for the faint of heart in any case, but I’d say that every serious .NET developer should read it at least once.

The Aristos

I saw this book in a random Twitter discussion and it caught my attention. I knew John Fowles by name only, but I haven’t read any of his more famous works. With that said I do enjoy this book so far. His personal point of view on religion, art, science and society are quite interesting.
