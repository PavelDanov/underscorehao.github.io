Key to Doing

看到和做到是两回事，

说到和做到是两回事。

理解和做到，仍旧是两回事。

不要把它们混为一谈。

这是做到的关键。

– Unknown author

My translation

Seeing and doing are two different things.

Saying and doing are two different things.

Understanding and doing are yet two different things.

Don’t confuse them.

This is the key to doing.
